Who We Are

Founded in 2026, Nebula is a full-stack career acceleration and placement support company dedicated to helping candidates build successful, long-term careers. We specialize in transforming job seekers into highly competitive professionals through a structured, end-to-end support system that covers every stage of the hiring journey ‚Äî from profile building to post-placement success.

With a team of 30+ key members, including technical experts, career mentors, and industry professionals, Nebula combines deep technical knowledge with real market insights to help candidates confidently step into their dream roles.

At Nebula, we provide complete career and placement solutions designed to make candidates job-ready and market-ready. Our services include:

  • Technical skill development and assessment support
  • Behavioral and technical interview preparation
  • Mock interviews and career coaching
  • Active job marketing and application management
